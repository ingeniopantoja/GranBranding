Free Consultation for Website built with Folio.

Read the below FAQ carefully before you submit your website for a consultation.

Why you may need this?

If you are reading this, It means you might have purchased a copy of Folio and you have completed building your site with the same. Now one scenario is when you/your developer completes building your brand/business site, unfortunately the final result doesn’t match the expectations. A little mistake on your website could have a big impact on your or your client's business. Therefore, we have come up with a free consultation service for our theme buyers.

Why do we do this?

The reason is very obvious – "Happy client, Happy us."

We take customer satisfaction very seriously, that’s why we always want to see the final result of your website as creative as Folio and we want our customers to grow with us. To fulfill this, We believe free consultation is the best way and we will provide you with real and truthful insights on where you can make improvements in your website or what did you forget by mistake :)

What includes?

One-time free consultation of your website built with Folio only.

We will review and evaluate your site with the following criteria in mind:

  • Design, alignment, spacing, color combination and visual hierarchy
  • Mobile readiness and mobile responsive
  • Overall performance, including page weight, page requests and page speed
  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
  • Security
  • Recommendations for Best Results

How it works?

After completion of your website development, create a ticket under Free Consultation category on our support portal. We will need your details like final website URL and optionally any specific message/notes you might have. We will review the site and will get back to you with final report with improvements/fixes needed.

What to do after contacting us?

Sit back, relax and let us do all of our work at this stage :) WAIT for a few hours or days. Good or bad – we’ll tell you the way it is. Your website may be visually and functionally accurate. If so, JUST GREAT! We’re transparent enough to let you know. If it really need some fixes, we’ll be sure to advise you on how to make it better. Either way, we’ll offer you honest answers as part of our free consultation.

Don'ts

  • Be patient after submitting your free consultation request through our Support Portal. Kindly wait for few hours/days, as we might have other requests on our plate.
  • If we receive multiple tickets to assess your site, we won't entertain other requests so make sure to create only one.
  • This is only a one-time free consultation service.
